Justification Memoranda

Per Secretarial Memo, Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) Framework for Directives, dated July 9, 2012, by September 1, 2012, all Justification Memoranda (JM) must be accompanied by a document reflecting an analysis using the ERM framework. Until this is incorporated into the JM it is recommended that all writers include the risk assessment tool below as an attachment for all JMs. (Templates and Tools are downloadable.)
