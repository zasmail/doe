ADAM MILES

Deputy Special Counsel, Policy and Congressional Affairs

Adam Miles is the deputy special counsel for policy and congressional affairs at the Office of Special Counsel. Mr. Miles is currently spearheading OSC’s work with whistleblowers to improve care at Department of Veteran Affairs medical centers across the country. Prior to joining OSC, Mr. Miles was on the staff of the House Committee on Oversight and Government Reform. Mr. Miles also worked at the D.C.-based non-profit, the Government Accountability Project.​ He is a graduate of Georgetown University Law Center and Boston University.​
